Professional & Public Life: Dave Birkett

As a sports journalist, Dave Birkett is a fixture in the NFL community, having covered the Detroit Lions as a beat writer for the Detroit Free Press since 2010. His work is recognized for its in-depth analysis and longevity, earning him the distinction of four-time Michigan Sportswriter of the Year.
